Orpheus is a website that helps artists design characters from scratch, gives out prompts and ideas to whoever needs it. It allows artists to pull themselves out of art block easily. Orpheus was the name of the most renowned artist of Greek mythology. As an archetype of the inspired singer, Orpheus is one of the most significant figures in the reception of classical mythology in Western culture, portrayed or alluded to in countless forms of art and popular culture including poetry, film, opera, music, and painting.
