Sometimes art block derives from a lack of inspiration, that’s why the best tip one could give an artist is to go out there and see the world. Using references is a practice as old as time and shouldn’t be frowned upon but, rather, used to its fullest potential. Finding artworks that inspire you is the first step to mastering your own style, the second is to practice, practice, practice. Here we have compiled a list of prompts you can follow every day of the month to practice as much as you can with a wide variety of subjetcs.

HELPFUL TIPS FOR CHARACTER DESIGN:

- Analyze famous character designs
- Practice world-building
-Break the character down in shapes
-Explore the character’s personality
through clothing and appearance
